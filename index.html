Interactive Lesson: A Boy Brushing His Teeth

📖 Story

A boy is brushing his teeth in the morning.

🔤 Main Vocabulary

  • brush teeth (đánh răng) /brʌʃ tiːθ/ 🔊
  • toothbrush (bàn chải đánh răng) /ˈtuːθ.brʌʃ/ 🔊
  • toothpaste (kem đánh răng) /ˈtuːθ.peɪst/ 🔊
  • bathroom (phòng tắm) /ˈbɑːθ.ruːm/ 🔊
  • sink (bồn rửa tay) /sɪŋk/ 🔊
  • mirror (gương) /ˈmɪr.ər/ 🔊
  • morning (buổi sáng) /ˈmɔː.nɪŋ/ 🔊

🧩 Descriptive Sentences

  • This is a boy. 🔊
  • He is brushing his teeth. 🔊
  • He is in the bathroom. 🔊
  • There is a mirror and a sink. 🔊
  • It is morning. 🔊

❓ Suggested Questions for Students

  • What is the boy doing?
  • Where is he?
  • What can you see in the bathroom?
  • Do you brush your teeth in the morning?
  • How many times do you brush your teeth every day?

✏️ Sample Description

This is a boy. He is in the bathroom. He is brushing his teeth. There is a mirror and a sink. It is morning.
